Biography

My name is Sarveshwar and I am currently a 12th grader attending Academy for Careers in Television and Film. Over this past summer of 2018, I completed the Bloomberg Arts and Culture Internship. It was a great experience being able to explore the city along with fellow interns and gaining proper work experience in various arts and culture institutions.

In my free time, I travel around the city doing photography as well as making short films about New York and have been doing so for almost 3 years. I post pictures of NYC daily to my Instagram account - @sunglassman as well as short films periodically to my YouTube account - EyesightFilms. I am often featured on many Instagram pages and have been recognized by many fellow photographers in New York City.
